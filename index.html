May 30, 2026 • Redding Town Green

A Community Celebration of America’s 250th Anniversary

Redding Celebrates America 250 is a special community event honoring the 250th anniversary of the founding of the United States.

The evening will feature a concert of patriotic music performed by the Greater Bridgeport Symphony, including works by Redding’s own Charles Ives, alongside popular American music performed in collaboration with students from Joel Barlow High School.

Set on the Redding Town Green, this free public concert brings together music, history, and community to mark an important national milestone.
